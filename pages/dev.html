Technology Overview

Our harvesting surfaces utilize advanced biomimetic coatings inspired by the Lotus Effect. This nanotechnology creates a high contact angle for water droplets, ensuring they flow instantly into collection channels without any absorption or evaporation losses.

Sustainable Impact

In arid regions, this system captures up to 45% more moisture compared to traditional methods. The self-cleaning properties prevent dust accumulation, maintaining peak performance year-round with zero maintenance.

UV-C & Ozone Sterilization

Sterilization99.9%

How it Works

This dual-stage system employs high-intensity UV-C radiation to disrupt the DNA of pathogens, followed by Ozone injection for deep oxidation. It effectively neutralizes viruses and bacteria without altering the water’s natural pH or mineral balance.

Environmental Benefit

By eliminating the need for chlorine or salts, we protect the soil’s microbial health, ensuring that the water provided to crops promotes natural growth and long-term land fertility.
