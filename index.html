ABOUT US

Preserving Every Single Drop

Rainwater harvesting is the systematic collection and utilization of rainwater from rooftops, open surfaces, and paved areas. Instead of being lost as surface runoff, this water can be stored, treated, and reused or allowed to recharge groundwater.
This website provides structured information on rainwater harvesting, focusing on collection methods, storage techniques, treatment processes, and practical applications. The aim is to promote efficient water management and sustainable use of rainwater in urban and rural environments.

Insights into recent developments in rainwater harvesting.
Development 01

Super-Hydrophobic Harvesting

Utilizing advanced super-hydrophobic materials to capture moisture directly from the atmocircle. This process maximizes water collection efficiency by minimizing surface friction, allowing micro-droplets to bead and flow instantly into collection reservoirs.

Key Tech: High-intensity UV LEDs, integrated ozone generator.

Super-Hydrophobic Harvesting
Development 02

Advanced UV-C & Ozone Sterilization

A powerful dual-stage purification module designed to eliminate 99.9% of waterborne pathogens. UV-C light disrupts microbial DNA, while integrated Ozone infusion provides a deep-clean finish, ensuring high-purity water without chemical additives.

Key Tech: High-intensity UV LEDs, integrated ozone generator.

Advanced UV-C & Ozone Sterilization

Development 03

IoT Predictive Distribution

An intelligent network management system that uses real-time sensors and AI. It predicts water demand based on environmental data and usage patterns, ensuring precise, automated delivery to specific architectural or agricultural zones.

Key Tech: Smart manifolds, AI-driven flow control, wireless sensor arrays.
